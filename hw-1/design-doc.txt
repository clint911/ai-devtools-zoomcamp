3. Typography
Fonts:
Headings: Montserrat Bold

Body Text: Open Sans

Accent Font: Bebas Neue (for unique messaging)

Sizing:
Task Title: 18px, #000000

Task Description: 14px, #000000

Unique Messaging: 24px, #FF6B35


4. Design Elements
Icons and Buttons:
Geometric shapes (triangles, diamonds, arrows) for icons.

Buttons: Rounded rectangles with bold borders, filled with Sunset Orange or Turquoise.

Patterns and Textures:
Subtle geometric patterns for card backgrounds or section dividers.

Layout:
Card-Based Tasks: Cream background, colored borders.

Spacing: Generous padding and margins.
